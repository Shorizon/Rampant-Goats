A guide on Rampant Goats Flashcards

This guide is here to help you navigate through the app.

Subjects: section describes all the subjects you can learn. Choose one subject to be quizzed on with flashcards dedicated to that subject. On the subject page,

Along with the subjects in the page, you will find an upload or create new flashcard button. This is where students, teacher and parents can create or upload their own flashcards.

Flashcards

When you use the flashcards:
- Press the “Answer” button, to reveal the answer to the question.
- Press “Next” button to go to the next question
- Press“Previous” button to go back to the previous question
- Progress bar displayed at the bottom of the screen shows your progress.
- The score tally will show how well you did in the flashcard quiz.

Account: This section allows the users to create their own account to store their own flashcards of chosen subject.
