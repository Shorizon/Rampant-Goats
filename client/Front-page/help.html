This guide is here to help you navigate through the app.
The “Categories” section describes all the subjects you can learn. Choose one to open a page with flashcards dedicated to that subject.
“Upload” section allows the user to create their own flashcards and add them to a chosen subject.
When you use the flashcards:
- Press the “Answer” button, to reveal the answer to the question.
- Press “Next” button to go to the next question
- Press“Previous” button to go back to the previous question
At the bottom of the screen you can see your progress.
At the end of each batch of flashcards you will see your score.
